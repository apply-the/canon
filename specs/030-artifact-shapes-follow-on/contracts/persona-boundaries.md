# Contract: Persona Boundaries

## Scope

This contract defines what persona guidance may and may not do in the 030
follow-on slice.

## Persona Mapping

| Mode | Persona | Intended Audience | Persona May Influence | Persona Must Not Influence |
|------|---------|-------------------|-----------------------|----------------------------|
| `discovery` | exploratory research lead | maintainers trying to frame an ambiguous problem space | exploratory clarity, opportunity framing, uncertainty language, and next-phase handoff readability | required sections, missing-gap handling, or evidence truthfulness |
| `system-shaping` | bounded system designer | maintainers reviewing domain boundaries and structural options | boundary emphasis, tradeoff language, rejection logic, and delivery sequencing readability | approval posture, invented architecture, or missing authored sections |
| `review` | skeptical packet reviewer | decision makers reviewing a bounded proposal packet | findings framing, severity language, evidence prioritization, and disposition readability | closure authority, evidence gaps, or final disposition honesty |

## Boundary Rules

- Persona guidance is advisory and presentational, not authoritative.
- Persona guidance cannot grant approval, close evidence gaps, or normalize a
  missing authored section into a complete packet.
- Any conflict between persona framing and Canon contract semantics resolves in
  favor of Canon contract semantics.
